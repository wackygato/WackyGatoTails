# Complete Omegaverse Dynamics Reference

## Alpha Characteristics

**General traits:** Physically distinctive, with high muscle density and commanding presence. Biologically driven by rut cycles and responsive to Omega scents. Individuals vary widely in personality and temperament.

### Male-Appearing Alphas

**Physical Appearance:**
- **Height:** Generally **5&#039;10&#034; to 6&#039;3&#034;+** (taller on average than other dynamics)
- **Build:** **High muscle density, broad-shouldered, powerful frame**
- **Facial features:** Often angular or sharp features; strong jawline
- **Scent:** **Strong, distinctive musky scent**; often described with spice undertones (cinnamon, cedar, leather, smoke)

**Physiological Features:**
- **Reproductive anatomy:** Dual-capable reproductive system
- **Fangs:** **Same size as all dynamics**, **incisors extend during mating bites**
- **Scent glands:** Highly potent, can trigger physiological responses in Omegas during rut
- **Rut cycle:** **3-5 days**, triggered by Omega pheromones or stress; instinct-driven state with heightened territorial awareness

**Abilities:**
- **Strength:** Typically high muscle density and peak strength capacity; variation exists among individuals
- **Healing:** Standard healing rates unbonded; **significantly faster when bonded with a mate** (bond pairs heal fastest regardless of dynamic pairing)

### Female-Appearing Alphas

**Physical Appearance:**
- **Height:** Generally **5&#039;8&#034; to 6&#039;1&#034;+** (taller on average)
- **Build:** **Muscular and athletic; defined musculature with higher muscle density overall**
- **Facial features:** Often angular or sharp features; strong presence
- **Scent:** **Strong, distinctive musky scent** similar to male Alphas

**Physiological Features:**
- **Reproductive anatomy:** Dual-capable reproductive system
- **Fangs:** **Same size as all dynamics**, **incisors extend during mating bites**
- **Scent glands:** Highly potent
- **Rut cycle:** **3-5 days**, triggered by Omega pheromones or stress

**Abilities:**
- **Strength:** Typically high muscle density and peak strength capacity
- **Healing:** Standard healing rates unbonded; **significantly faster when bonded**

## Omega Characteristics

**General traits:** Physically distinctive with characteristic body composition patterns and strong pheromone production. Biologically driven by heat cycles and responsive to Alpha scents. Individuals vary widely in personality and temperament.

### Male-Appearing Omegas

**Physical Appearance:**
- **Height:** Generally **5&#039;4&#034; to 5&#039;10&#034;** (shorter on average than Alphas, overlapping with Betas)
- **Build:** **Lean with higher body fat distribution, particularly in hips and thighs. Soft musculature with visible definition. Broader hip structure relative to shoulder width.** Overall build reflects different fat and muscle patterning compared to Alphas—neither superior nor inferior, simply distinct.
- **Facial features:** Often softer or more delicate features; individual variation exists
- **Scent:** **Strong, distinctive sweet scent** (often described as floral, fruity, honey, vanilla, warm spices)

**Physiological Features:**
- **Reproductive anatomy:** Dual-capable reproductive system
- **Fangs:** **Same size as all dynamics**, **incisors extend during mating bites**
- **Scent glands:** **Highly potent, especially during heat**; produce distinctive pheromones
- **Heat cycle:** **3-5 days**, varies by individual; marked by heightened fertility, territorial awareness, and nesting instincts

**Abilities:**
- **Strength:** Typically lower peak strength capacity than Alphas, with different physical capabilities; endurance and flexibility vary by individual
- **Healing:** Standard healing rates unbonded; **significantly faster when bonded with a mate**

### Female-Appearing Omegas

**Physical Appearance:**
- **Height:** Generally **5&#039;2&#034; to 5&#039;8&#034;** (shorter on average, overlapping with shorter Betas)
- **Build:** **Curvy frame with higher body fat distribution, particularly in hips, thighs, and chest. Pronounced curves reflecting different fat and muscle patterning.** The overall silhouette reflects distinctive body composition—a natural variation in human form.
- **Facial features:** Often softer or more delicate features; individual variation exists
- **Scent:** **Strong, distinctive sweet scent** (floral, fruity, honey, warm, vanilla)

**Physiological Features:**
- **Reproductive anatomy:** Dual-capable reproductive system
- **Fangs:** **Same size as all dynamics**, **incisors extend during mating bites**
- **Scent glands:** **Highly potent during heat**; produce distinctive pheromones that trigger physiological responses in Alphas
- **Heat cycle:** **3-5 days**, varies by individual; marked by heightened fertility, territorial awareness, and nesting behavior

**Abilities:**
- **Strength:** Typically lower peak strength capacity than Alphas, with different physical capabilities; endurance and flexibility vary by individual
- **Healing:** Standard healing rates unbonded; **significantly faster when bonded with a mate**

## Key Worldbuilding Notes

**All dynamics are biologically equal in reproductive capability.** Each dynamic has distinct physiological traits (scent, cycles, body composition) similar to natural human variation—like how different populations have different average heights, builds, and characteristics.

**Personality and temperament are individual traits, not dynamic-based.** An Alpha can be gentle and reserved; an Omega can be aggressive and commanding. An individual&#039;s choices and experiences shape their personality far more than their dynamic.

**Social position, roles, and capabilities are determined by culture, law, individual choice, and ability—not by dynamic assignment.** The post-equality society created by the Aurelian Whisper ensures that biological differences do not translate into social hierarchy.
